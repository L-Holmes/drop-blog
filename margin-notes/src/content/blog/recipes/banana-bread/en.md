There's a particular kind of comfort in a loaf that asks very little of you. No mixer, no special tins, no waiting for butter to soften. Just a bowl, a spoon, and three bananas that have gone too far to eat on their own.

## What you need

- 3 very ripe bananas
- 100g butter, melted (or any mild oil)
- 150g sugar
- 2 eggs
- 200g plain flour
- 1 tsp baking soda
- a pinch of salt

## How to make it

1. Heat the oven to 180°C and line a loaf tin.
2. Mash the bananas in a big bowl until mostly smooth.
3. Stir in the butter, sugar and eggs.
4. Add the flour, baking soda and salt, and mix just until it comes together.
5. Pour into the tin and bake for about 50 minutes, until a knife comes out clean.

> The browner and uglier the bananas, the better the bread.

Let it cool a little before slicing — if you can wait that long. That's the whole thing.
