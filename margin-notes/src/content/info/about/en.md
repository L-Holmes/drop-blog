Margin Notes is a small, independent blog written by ordinary people — parents, partners, friends — sharing what's actually worked for us on normal, busy days.

We're not experts and we don't pretend to be. We test things ourselves, we tell you the downsides, and we never take payment to recommend something.

If a post helped, that's the whole point. If it didn't, tell us — there's a real person at the other end.
